# Argonautica

**Fifty players on one ship. A woman who does not have to help him, and does. And then he leaves her.**

Story #9 — the era **`the-golden-fleece`**, which hands on to **`the-labyrinth *(unwritten — the story ends here)*`**.

```kotlin
compileOnly("net.crewco:mythos-addon-api:0.1.3")
```

## The mechanic worth knowing

**The first chapter that does not work without a crowd.** `/power sail` moves *every player standing
within fourteen blocks of the ship*, together, across the sea. Everyone who wandered off to look at something
is left on the beach watching it go — which is exactly what happened to Heracles, and he never caught up.

**Medea can say no.** She is a player, under no obligation whatsoever. The ointment is the only reason Jason
survives the bulls; the dragon has never slept and she is the only thing that can put it to sleep. **If she
declines, the fleece stays on its tree and the addon narrates that ending without a word of complaint.**

It is also the only era whose epilogue **takes a side**.
