# Argonautica — Roles

| id | who | tier | seats | how you get it |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `jason` | Jason | `HERO` | 1 | completes every task and personally solves none of them |
| `medea` | Medea | `DEMIGOD` | 1 | **nothing in this story works without her, and everyone in it treats her as a complication** |
| `argonaut` | An Argonaut | `HERO` | 50 | you rowed. You are in the poem as a *name*, in a list, once — and that is better than almost anybody gets. |
| `aeetes` | Aeëtes | `MORTAL` | 1 | has the fleece, a dragon, and a daughter who is cleverer than he is |

> A power missing from a role's `powers` list **does not exist** — `PowerService` checks it
> before firing. See [Powers](https://github.com/Crew-co/Mythos/wiki/Powers).
